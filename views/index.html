    MEET YOUR REMOTE TEAM

    Awesome OS helps growing startups build exceptional outsourced teams through outsourcing focused on partnership, with exceptional people at a competitive price, high-touch service from our expert team, and flexible contracts.

    Founded in 2006 by a leadership team with over 50 years of combined outsourcing experience, we know what leads to long-term success, and we’re here to help you get there.

    OUR PEOPLE

    Our people are our greatest asset and our driving force. Through rigorous hiring standards and a strong focus on culture fit, we find the best candidates in the industry. Then we provide them with the training, technology, and environments that they need to thrive. We focus on empowerment, recognition, and growth.

    Employees get a full suite of benefits including competitive salaries, paid time off, investment plans, health insurance, team-building events, access to sports and fitness activities, and even nap rooms and massages. It’s no wonder our average annual attrition rate is just 15% (WAY lower than industry averages).

    The result is engaged, proactive employees who are passionate about their work.

    OUR PARTNERSHIP

    Our team is your team. You get access to our hands-on leadership team with deep experience in customer support, call centers, and back office outsourcing. Even our CEO and COO are available to advise at any time, and a U.S. based account manager will help you with anything you need to make your program thrive.

    Flexible: 24/7 coverage with no annual contract, no headcount minimums, and no annual increase minimums. Ramp up quickly and hire new team members within days. No one-size-fits-all programs here. We’ll work with you to build the perfect solution for you.

    Affordable: Pay per agent with low, all-inclusive rates -- no upcharge for overnight shifts or phone support. Save up to 80% as compared to an in-house hire and get equal or better quality.

    Integrated: We’ll work with you to make sure your professionals understand your product, values, and culture so they can act as a seamless extension of your overall team. You’ll have full visibility into team operation and complete access to communicate with your employees directly.

    OUR LOCATION

    Lower cost of living means lowering operating costs, and a huge talent pool of educated and passionate people means top-notch results.

    The Philippines is the third-largest English speaking country in the world. Filipinos speak English with little to no accent and have a strong understanding of Western culture -- so much so that the Philippines has been named as the global leader in quality of English and Western cultural affinity. *According to the Talent Index generated by O2P and the Everest Group.

    Davao is the third-most populated city in the Philippines. With millions of qualified professionals and fewer outsourcers than other Philippine cities, there’s less saturation and more access to workforce. Solid infrastructure, skilled people, and local government support for the industry make Davao the perfect location.
